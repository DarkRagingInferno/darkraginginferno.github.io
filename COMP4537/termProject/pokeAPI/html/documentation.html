poke-amar-john is a web api that tries to capture a bit of the magic of pokemon we all know and love.

https://pokeapi-amar-john.herokuapp.com/api/v1

In order to use this API you must sign up and create a trainer in order to use this service.

Afterwords, simply just login

GET

/login

Login to your Trainer

Pokemon

Pokemon are creatures of all shapes and sizes who live in the wild or alongside humans

>

Pokemon Endpoints

GET

/pokemon

Gets a Pokemon

PUT

/pokemon/update

Updates a pokemon's properties such as [name, hp, attack, generation, etc...]

DELETE

/pokemon/delete

Deletes a pokemon

POST

/pokemon/create

Creates a new pokemon

>

Trainers

Trainers is a person who catches, trains, cares for, and battles with Pokémon. The majority of people within the known Pokémon world are Trainers

Trainer Endpoints

GET

/trainer

Gets a Trainer

PUT

/trainer/update

Updates a Trainer's information [first name, last name, etc...]

DELETE

/trainer/delete

Deletes a Trainer

POST

/trainer/create

Creates a new Trainer

POST

/trainer/image

Posts an image to a Trainer

Party

Parties consists of up to 6 pokemon a Trainer brings on their journey.

Party Endpoints

GET

/party

Gets a party

GET

/party/id

Gets a party id

PUT

/party/add

Adds a pokemon to a party

DELETE

/party/delete

Deletes a Pokemon from a party

POST

/party/create

Creates a new party
